Use Case Name:Hotel Registration


Participating Actor(s):HotelUser,Registrar


Entry Condition(s):User selects "Hotel Registration" option in "Register" use case.
Exit Condition(s):System initiates "Approve Registration" use case.


Priority:High

FLow of Events:


1)After the selection in entry condition system displays "Hotel Registration Form".
	2)"HotelUser" fills the "Hotel Registration Form" and clicks on "Complete Button".

3)System sends the "Hotel Registration Form" to "Manage Registration" use case and displays "HotelRegisterSuccess" message


Objects:"Hotel Registration Form","Hotel User","Complete Button","HotelRegisterSuccessMessage".
